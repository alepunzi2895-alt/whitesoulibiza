Our Story

10 years on the island.
Every contact built by hand.

We have been based in Ibiza for over a decade. In that time we have built direct relationships with the island's finest villa owners, renowned restaurants, private clubs, yacht captains and event professionals.

Those relationships are the real product. When you come to us, you are not paying for a booking platform — you are gaining access to a network that took ten years to build, offered to you as a single seamless service.

Our team are locals and long-time islanders who know every corner of the island. We know which cove is empty at noon, which chef is worth the drive, which table to ask for. Ibiza, properly known — and entirely yours.

Philosophy

Luxury is attention.

Luxury is not about excess. It is about attention — to the temperature of the room, the wine in the glass, the moment you walk into the kitchen and find exactly what you wanted at exactly the right time.

That kind of attention cannot be automated, scaled, or franchised. It can only be given by a team that cares. That is what we are.

If you are looking for a transaction, there are many concierges in Ibiza. If you are looking for a relationship — for someone to hold your stay with thought and discretion — we would love to meet you.
