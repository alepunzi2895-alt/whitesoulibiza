Our Services

Tailor-made, end to end.

A single point of contact orchestrating every element of your Ibiza stay — from arrival to last lingering sunset.

01 / Concierge
Villa Concierge

Your home, anticipated.

From the moment your villa is booked, our team begins quietly preparing the rooms you'll move through. Linen turned, pantries stocked to your taste, transfers timed to the minute, and a discreet manager on call through every hour of your stay.

  • Pre-arrival villa setup & personalised provisioning
  • Dedicated 24/7 concierge & in-residence host
  • Airport, helicopter and yacht transfers
  • Housekeeping, laundry, butler & security
  • Childcare, pet care, fitness trainers on request
02 / Cuisine
Private Chef in Villa

A table made for you.

Michelin-trained chefs arrive at your villa with a vision built from a single conversation. Mediterranean tasting menus, Japanese omakase, family-style suppers under the olive trees — every dish paired, every wine considered, every plate cleared without sound.

  • Personalised menus shaped to dietary and cultural preferences
  • Sommelier-curated wine pairings & rare-bottle sourcing
  • Live cooking experiences & cocktail mixology
  • Daily breakfast service & in-villa baker
  • Private tastings, themed nights & chef tables
03 / Sea
Yacht & Boat Experiences

Beyond the shoreline.

The Balearics unfold differently from the water. Sail to Formentera at first light, drop anchor at Cala Saladeta for lunch from the kitchen, return to your villa as the cliffs catch fire. Day cruises, overnight charters, or week-long voyages — captained by our trusted crew.

  • Sailing yachts, motor yachts, gulets & day-cruisers
  • Crewed charters with chef, captain & hostess
  • Formentera day trips & sunset itineraries
  • Water-sports: jet ski, paddle, foiling & scuba
  • Beach club arrivals & Cala Jondal reservations
04 / Access
VIP Services & Reservations

Doors that open quietly.

Ibiza's most coveted tables, terraces, and front-row tables — held in our name, given to you. We don't queue, we don't wait, and neither do you.

  • VIP tables: Ushuaïa, Pacha, Lío, Hï, DC10
  • Beach club privileges: Cipriani, Beso, Amante, Cotton
  • Restaurant reservations across the island
  • Helicopter, supercar & vintage vehicle rentals
  • Private DJ & artist bookings
05 / Wellness
Wellness & Experiences

A quieter kind of luxury.

The island has long been a place of healing. We work with Ibiza's most respected therapists, yogis, and breath-work guides — designing rituals around your needs, in the privacy of your villa or on a cliff at sunrise.

  • In-villa massage, facials & holistic treatments
  • Private yoga, pilates & breath-work sessions
  • Sound baths, ceremony & ritual experiences
  • Personal trainers, runners & padel coaches
  • Nutritionists, IV therapy & doctors on call
